Governance

10:10 Academy is a 501(c)(3) non-profit youth organization. We are overseen by an impartial Board of Directors comprised of qualified advisors for our specific mission and vision. These include pastors, educational professionals, social workers, business owners, those with extensive non-profit experience, homeschool families, and 10:10 Academy community members.

To request more information about our Board of Directors, or an annual report, please email Info@1010Academy.com.
