About Us

10:10 Academy is a Christ-focused Collaborative Learning Community (CLC) based in Riverside, California. We offer weekly academic and elective classes for students K-12. Our program is designed to minister to the whole person by creating a loving learning environment with Biblical standards. We offer opportunities not just for learning, but to connect and build meaningful relationships for the whole family.
